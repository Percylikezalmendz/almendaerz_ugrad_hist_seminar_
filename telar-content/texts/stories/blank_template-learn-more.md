Holly was a multimillion dollar project created in Louisiana and transported to California in 1966. The 1950s-1960s Santa Barbara/Ventura Coastline saw a rapid expansion in the local oil industry, with the first oil platforms being established in 1958-59 off the coast of Summerland. One of these original platforms (Platform Habitat) in Summerland actually caught fire recently on May 11th, 2026.
